## Investment Philosophy

### Core Principle
**"Buy the Dip, Hold for Wealth"** - Deploy lump sum capital only during market corrections to maximize long-term returns while managing downside risk.

### When to Invest: Same Day vs Next Day?

| Scenario | Recommendation | Reason |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| Market falls 3-5% in a day | **Next Trading Day** | Allow volatility to settle, avoid catching a falling knife |
| Market falls gradually over weeks | **Same Day** | Correction is already priced in |
| Global event triggers fall | **Wait 2-3 Days** | Events need time to fully reflect |
| India-specific correction | **Same Day** | Local corrections recover faster |

> **Pro Tip:** For mutual funds, place orders before 1:30 PM to get same-day NAV. For ETFs, buy during market hours when spread is tight (11 AM - 2 PM is optimal).
