## POLICY 1 — 2 Cr (MAIN POLICY WITH RIDERS)

### Riders (MUST HAVE — Only on This Policy)

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Waiver of Premium | Is it included? What is the cost? | **YES** (~1k–2k/year) | **YES** |
| Critical Illness Cover | Sum assured? Payout trigger? | **YES** (20–25L, payout on diagnosis) | **YES** |
| Diseases Covered under CI | Exact list of diseases | Cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, organ transplant (minimum) | **YES** |
| CI Survival Period | How many days must you survive after diagnosis to claim? | Typically 30 days — **confirm and get in writing** | **YES** |
| Rider Premium Breakup | Is the cost of each rider shown separately? | **YES** — base premium + each rider cost must be itemized | **YES** |
| Accidental Death Benefit | Is additional accidental cover available/needed? | Optional — evaluate based on occupation/lifestyle | No |
| Rider Tenure | Do riders last the full policy term or expire earlier? | **Must match policy term or be clearly stated** | **YES** |

### Basics

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Pure term plan? | No return of premium, no maturity benefit | **YES** | **YES** |
| Premium fixed for full term? | Will it change after year 1? | **NO — fixed for entire term** | **YES** |
| Premium amount from year 2 onwards | Confirm exact amount in writing | Must be **clearly defined and identical** to year 1 | **YES** |
| Policy term | Duration of coverage | **Till age 65–70** | **YES** |
| Sum Assured | Total death benefit | **2 Crore** | **YES** |
| Premium Payment Frequency | Monthly / Quarterly / Annual | Annual recommended (avoids lapse risk) | Recommended |

### Medical & Disclosure

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Full medical tests required? | Blood work, ECG, BP, BMI, urine | **YES — all must be done** | **YES** |
| Pre-existing conditions | Must disclose diabetes, BP, thyroid, etc. | **Disclose everything** | **YES** |
| Smoking / tobacco / alcohol | Current or past usage | **Must disclose truthfully** | **YES** |
| Existing insurance policies | All current life/health policies | **Must disclose all** | **YES** |
| Family medical history | Parents/siblings history of major illness | **Disclose if asked** | **YES** |

### Claim Safety

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Claim settlement ratio (1Cr+ claims) | Not overall ratio — ask specifically for high-value claims | **97–99%** | **YES** |
| Section 45 (fraud protection) | After how many years can the insurer not contest the policy? | **3 years** — after this, claim cannot be denied except for fraud | **YES** |
| Average claim settlement time | How many days from filing to payout? | **Less than 30 days** is ideal | Recommended |

### Exclusions (CRITICAL — Get in Writing)

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Written exclusion list | Full list of what is NOT covered | **Must be provided in writing** | **YES** |
| Suicide clause | Is there a waiting period? | Typically **1 year exclusion** — confirm | **YES** |
| Hazardous activities | Adventure sports, racing, etc. | **Must be clearly defined** | **YES** |
| Alcohol/smoking-related death | Covered if disclosed at purchase? | **YES — if disclosed truthfully** | **YES** |
| Death outside India | Is the nominee covered if death occurs abroad? | **Must be covered** — confirm | **YES** |

### Nominee & Assignment

| Item | What to Ask / Verify | Expected Answer | Mandatory |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Nominee name & relationship | Correctly recorded? | **YES** | **YES** |
| Can nominee be changed later? | Process for updating | **YES — must be simple** | **YES** |
| Policy assignment for loan | Can the policy be assigned to a bank? | Confirm process | Recommended |

### FINAL CHECK — POLICY 1

- [ ] Riders included and itemized
- [ ] Full medical tests completed
- [ ] Premium is fixed for entire term
- [ ] All exclusions received in writing
- [ ] Nominee details verified
- [ ] Benefit illustration received

## COMMON QUESTIONS — ASK FOR ALL 3 POLICIES

### Premium & Payment

| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| Grace period for missed premium? | **30 days** |
| What happens if premium is missed beyond grace? | **Policy lapses** |
| Revival after lapse? | **Possible with conditions (medical + arrears)** |
| Is there a premium payment lock-in? | Confirm — some policies have minimum payment years |

### Free Look Period (IMPORTANT — Often Missed)

| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| Free look period available? | **YES — 15 to 30 days** after receiving policy document |
| Full refund if cancelled during free look? | **YES** (minus medical/stamp charges) |

> **Use this period to read the full policy document. Cancel if anything is different from what was promised.**

### Tax Benefits

| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| Section 80C deduction on premium? | **YES** (up to 1.5L combined limit) |
| Section 10(10D) on death benefit? | **YES — death benefit is tax-free for nominee** |

| Online claim filing available? | **YES** |
